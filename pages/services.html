What We Offer

Individual Coaching

One-on-one sessions are the foundation of our coaching. We'll analyze your technique, identify key areas for improvement, and build a clear plan to help you reach your bowling goals. All our individual coaching sessions include a range of tools and techniques to support your development.

  • Complete form analysis
  • Personalized drills and exercises
  • Mental game coaching
  • Lane play strategies
  • Equipment recommendations
  • Video feedback and analysis
  • Equipment fitting guidance
  • Mental-game coaching modules
  • Package deals available at discounted rates

All of these elements can be customized based on your needs. Discuss which options work best for your goals during your consultation or first lesson.

Who We Coach

Beginners

New to bowling? We'll teach you the fundamentals from the ground up, including proper grip, stance, approach, and release. We'll build a solid foundation that sets you up for long-term success.

Perfect for: First-time bowlers, casual players looking to improve

Youth Bowlers

Specializing in youth development, we make learning fun while building proper technique. We focus on age-appropriate instruction that keeps young bowlers engaged and excited about the sport.

Perfect for: Elementary through high school students

Intermediate/Advanced

Already bowling regularly? We'll help you break through plateaus, refine your technique, and develop advanced strategies for reading lanes and making adjustments during competition.

Perfect for: League bowlers, tournament competitors

Specialized Techniques

One-Handed Style

Master the traditional one-handed approach with focus on consistency, accuracy, and power. We'll help you develop proper wrist position, release timing, and follow-through for maximum effectiveness.

Two-Handed Style

Learn the modern two-handed technique that's revolutionizing the sport. We'll teach you the unique footwork, timing, and release mechanics needed to generate powerful rev rates with this style.
