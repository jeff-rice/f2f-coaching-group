About F2F Coaching Group

Our Mission

To develop confident, competitive bowlers at all levels through personalized, fundamentals-driven coaching that emphasizes consistency, smart adjustments, and long-term success on and off the lanes.

We believe no two bowlers are the same. Every athlete deserves a customized coaching approach rooted in encouragement, support, and purposeful practice. We help bowlers build strong foundations and finish strong when it matters most.

Meet Our Coaches

Award-winning, certified coaches dedicated to your success.

Jeff Rice

Coach & Founder

🏆 USBC Silver Level Certified

⭐ 2022-2023 Indy USBC Youth Coach of the Year

✓ RVP Certified & SafeSport Compliant

Jeff brings a lifelong passion for bowling and a proven track record of developing individual athletes at the middle school, high school, and regional levels. Known for his personalized, fundamentals-driven approach, Jeff is committed to helping every bowler build confidence, consistency, and peak performance.

Highlights: Head Coach of Southport High School Bowling (2022–Present), Head Coach/Coordinator for Middle School Bowling (2021–Present). Coached a four-time Individual Sectionals Champion and a U15 USA Bowling Regional Runner-Up team during the 2025–2026 season.

Specialties: Video analysis, personalized technique development, all skill levels and ages, competitive player training.

Rob Pero

Coach & Co-Founder

🏆 USBC Silver Level Certified

⭐ 2023-2024 Indy USBC Youth Coach of the Year

✓ RVP Certified & SafeSport Compliant

Rob is a USBC Silver Level Certified Coach whose philosophy emphasizes smart adjustments, confident decision-making, and the development of complete, uniquely-styled competitors. Actively coaching since 2019, Rob works with bowlers from ages 5 to 70+, building confidence through encouragement, support, and purposeful practice.

Highlights: Head Coach and Program Lead at Franklin Central High School, Indy East Conference Coordinator, All Indianapolis Coordinator, Task Force Member with Indiana High School Bowling, Director with the Indy USBC, and contributor to BowlIndy youth initiatives.

Specialties: Youth and high school programs, team coaching, building confidence and sportsmanship, individualized skill assessment.

Our Community Impact

Beyond individual coaching, we're dedicated to strengthening bowling across Indiana.

Our coaches lead and develop high school bowling programs at Southport and Franklin Central, fostering championship-level competition and athlete development.

We actively support youth bowling initiatives through coaching, program coordination, and tournament organization at the local and state levels.

As Directors and coordinators with the Indy USBC, we help advance bowling through member relations, youth initiatives, and tournament coordination.
