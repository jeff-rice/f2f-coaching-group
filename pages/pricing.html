If you miss the rescheduled consultation, you will no longer be eligible for a free consultation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is lane rental included?

Yes, lane rental is included in all standard lesson rates ($130 adult, $120 student). If you prefer to provide your own lane time, ask about our BYOL coaching-only rates.

What are the package options?

Package deals are available at discounted rates. Discuss package options during your free consultation or first lesson to find a plan that works for you.

Do you coach youth and adults?

Absolutely! We offer specialized coaching for both youth bowlers (18 and under) and adults. Youth rates are $10 less per hour.

Can I use my own lane time?

Yes! If you have lane time covered through a league or elsewhere, ask about our BYOL (Bring Your Own Lane Time) coaching-only rates.

How do I get started?

New clients can book a free consultation to discuss your goals and assess your technique. No commitment required!

How do I book?

Visit our Contact Us page to schedule your consultation or first lesson.
