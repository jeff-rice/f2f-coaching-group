Frequently Asked Questions

How long is a typical coaching session?

A typical coaching session is 1-hour in length.

What can I expect from my first lesson?

During your first lesson, we'll work on fundamentals, assess your current technique, and begin building a personalized coaching plan based on your goals.

Do you coach youth and adults?

Yes! We offer specialized coaching for both youth bowlers (18 and under) and adults.

Do I need my own bowling ball and shoes?

It's helpful to have your own equipment, but it's not required. We can work with house equipment as well.

Do I need to book a consultation before scheduling a lesson?

We recommend a consultation for first-time clients so we can discuss your goals and ensure we're a good fit. Returning clients can book lessons directly.

What are the coaching rates?

Visit our Pricing page for detailed information on all our coaching packages and rates.
